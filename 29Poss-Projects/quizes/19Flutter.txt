3. What is the difference between Hot Reload and Hot Restart in Flutter?

Answer: Hot Reload preserves app state, injects updated source code, faster (~1 second), maintains current screen and data. Hot Restart destroys app state, restarts app completely, slower (~4 seconds), returns to initial screen. Use Hot Reload for UI changes, Hot Restart for state model changes.
